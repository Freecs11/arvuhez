Fête de la Nature in Bretagne
From May 18 to 22, the Fête de la Nature is an opportunity to live a unique and free experience in contact with nature: guided tours of protected sites, accompanied walks in large protected natural areas, exploration of marine environments, inventories of participatory species, treasure hunts…
get ready to discover the nature of Brittany from a new angle!


May 22 also happens to be the International biodiversity Day.
