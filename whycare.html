Linsday Stalzer
These athletes are playing for the planet.
Athletes Unlimited launched Athlete Causes in partnership with Give Lively and the Give Lively Foundation to develop athletes as civic leaders and elevate them as role models for the next generation. 1% for the Planet is just one of many nonprofit beneficiary partners that players can choose to support during their season.
This initiative allows athletes to play their season in part for the benefit of the nonprofit organization of their choice. At the end of the season, the Give Lively Foundation will make a grant equal to 50% of the athlete’s end-of-season bonus to the nonprofit.

Why Should We Care About the Environment?
that's a fair question to ask , and it's very essential to understand the mouvements and all the events promoting this idea of taking care of our environment. Now to start, as you know the environment around us is an essential part of our survival. I like to believe that people who do not care about the environment, simply do not understand how important it is to all of us and think that it does not affect them directly in their day to day life and i disagree with that assumption, these are my reasons you should be concerned about the environment.

A Healthy life needs a 'Healthy' Environment :


many people aren't aware of this , but a lot of diseases are caused by an unclean environment, the more we don't take care of our environment , the more it becomes polluted with contaminants and toxins who have huge impacts on our health, take for exemple air pollution, it can cause respiratory diseases and cancer and those are just the top on the iceberg it causes alot more problems that are even more dangerous, another exemple would be cater pollution, etc.

Global warming:


yes this one is a little tricky, yes it does not affect you directly, what it will affect if it's left is your children and their lives and their future.

Importance of Biodiversity:


Biodiversity refers to the variety of plants, animals, and other living things in our world. it can be negatively influenced by habitat loss and degradation due to human activity, climate change, and pollution, among other things.
