These athletes are playing for the planet
SEPTEMBER 15, 2021
Linsday Stalzer
These athletes are playing for the planet.
Athletes Unlimited launched Athlete Causes in partnership with Give Lively and the Give Lively Foundation to develop athletes as civic leaders and elevate them as role models for the next generation. 1% for the Planet is just one of many nonprofit beneficiary partners that players can choose to support during their season.
This initiative allows athletes to play their season in part for the benefit of the nonprofit organization of their choice. At the end of the season, the Give Lively Foundation will make a grant equal to 50% of the athlete’s end-of-season bonus to the nonprofit.
