Global energy consomption: a major stake for our environnement’s future.
Global warming always is the first think people think of when you tell them « global environnemental issues ».

And while it is for very good reasons, global warming(along with the melting of the glacier and the global rising water that it directly provokes) effectively being a major issue for the future of our environment, hence of our civilisation. The thing is, our temperatures are rising up for several reasons, one of witch being our consomption of energy.
And if Global warming is the problem of where our energy « goes », other problem worth discussing are : where does it come from(is sustainable in long term) and what do we do with it(are we wasting it?).

In our current way of life, we use numerical technoligies a lot, we drive cars, we warm up our houses when it’s cold etc.
All of those commodities consumes a lot of energy and, we depend heavily on them, wich mean we depend heavily on energies that are still largely produced by fossile ressources as of today(coal and gas being the first two ressources we use to make electricity),
wich are not suitable in long term because of limited, rapidily diminishinf natural supplies.

This means that, even if solutions(like nuclear fusion) exist and are currently researched, we might have, as a society and as indivduals,
become less dependent on high consumming products and services, before we have to cut them out completely.
