Louis Beamois take on campus problems
The main environmental problem that affects me on a daily basis is the waste, that we can come across everywhere, whether on the college campus or even elsewhere. Personally in my free time I kayak and every time I pick up what floats on the water because I find it very unpleasant to spend time and have fun in a "dirty" place.
Unfortunately I can't spend my time picking up everything that I come across as waste… I do it mostly on the water.

I know that several actions are proposed each year to clean up the rivers, for example the association “Yves Rochers” has already done it many times in Brittany, but I think we shouldn't wait for someone to do it for us. Everyone should stop throwing things anywhere like that, so no one should clean up the damage of others and we would live in a healthier environment and therefore I am also convinced that everyone would be happier on a daily basis to live in clean places.
