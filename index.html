WHO ARE WE ?
Ar Vuhez is a student and college association for environmental awareness. It encourages the exchange of knowledge, skills and experience.
OUR VALUES
The environment is nature, but not only! It is also all the people around us and the relationship we have with them.
 
Ar Vuhez therefore promotes citizen and collective initiatives. Its organization is “horizontal” and non-hierarchical. Everyone has the right to launch a project, to speak openly and to be listened to. The association promotes participatory actions to encourage individual responsibility within a collective project.
